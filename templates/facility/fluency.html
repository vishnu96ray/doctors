Fluency Therapy

Fluency therapy or stuttering therapy Clinic in Delhi

A fluency disorder, which is often referred to as “stuttering”, is characterized by primary (core) and secondary behaviours. Primary behaviours may include repetitions of sounds, syllables, or whole words; prolongations of single sounds; or blocks of airflow or voicing during speech.

Lat's Talk Clinic offers individual, group and intensive group treatment programs for adults and teens with fluency disorder to find ways to improve the fluency of their speech and reduce the impact stammering has on their lives.
