BERA Hearing Test

BERA “OR” Brainstem Evoked Response Audiometry

BERA hearing test clinic in delhi

BERA Hearing Test or say Brainstem Evoked Response Audiometry or Auditory brain stem response or an ABR audiometry or BAER or Brainstem auditory evoked response audiometry in children with hearing loss and delayed speech is a neurophysiological method for the evaluation of the hearing threshold and diagnosing retrocochlear lessons.

It’s an objective way of eliciting brain stem potentials in response to audiological click stimuli which should be always viewed in conjunction with other audiological investigations.
