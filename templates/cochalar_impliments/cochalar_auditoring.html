Auditory Verbal Therapy for teaching deaf children to listen and speak

Auditory verbal therapy is a method for teaching deaf children to listen and speak and emphasizes speech and listening.

Constant use of amplification devices such as hearing aids, cochlear implants etc helps the patients to listen while using their residual hearing power.
