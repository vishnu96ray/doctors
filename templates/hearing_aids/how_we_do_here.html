How Do We Hear

Components of Ear that helps us in hearing.

When the Sound Waves enter the ear canal and cause the eardrum to vibrate, that vibration pass through three connected bones in the middle ear. All this motion sets moving fluid in the inner ear. Finally it reaches to the processing center that translates it to meaningful audio.

There are five components of ear as follows:

  • Outer Ear
  • Middle Ear
  • Inner Ear
  • Acoustic Nerve
